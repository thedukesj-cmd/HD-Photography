We are thrilled to announce that registration for the **2024 Aperture Annual Exhibition** is now open to all club members.

## Theme: Thresholds

This year's theme, *Thresholds*, invites photographers to explore the idea of transitions — doorways, coastlines, dawn and dusk, the edge of a forest, the border between two worlds. We are looking for images that capture the moment between one state and another.

## Submission Guidelines

- **Maximum 3 photographs per member**
- All genres welcome: landscape, portrait, street, abstract, macro
- Minimum resolution: 3000px on the longest edge
- Submit as JPEG or TIFF, Adobe RGB or sRGB colour profile
- All images must be your own original work, taken after January 1, 2023

## Key Dates

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| March 1 – April 15 | Submission window |
| April 22 | Jury decisions announced |
| May 3 | Exhibition opening night (members + guests) |
| May 4 – June 2 | Public exhibition at the Meridian Gallery |

## Prizes

- **Best in Show**: £500 + a one-year Prime membership
- **Judges' Choice** (3 awards): £150 each
- **People's Choice**: Selected by public vote during the exhibition

Submit your work through the member portal. Questions? Contact exhibitions@apertureclub.org.

We look forward to seeing your vision of *Thresholds*.
