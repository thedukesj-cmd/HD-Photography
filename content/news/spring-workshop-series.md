We've listened to your feedback from last year's survey, and we're thrilled to announce our most ambitious workshop programme yet.

## Spring 2024 Workshop Calendar

### March 16 — Golden Hour Landscape
Led by **Elena Vasquez** at Cliffs of Moher National Park. Limited to 12 participants. Covers planning, composition, and long-exposure techniques. Full day, 5am–8pm. Cost: £85 members / £120 non-members.

### April 6 — Portrait Lighting Fundamentals
Led by **Sarah O'Brien** in our studio. Half day, 10am–2pm. Covers one-light, two-light, and natural light setups. Cost: £65 members / £90 non-members.

### April 20 — Street Photography Walk: Dublin City Centre
Led by **James Chen**. Half day, 2pm–7pm. Walk and shoot through Temple Bar, the Liberties, and Smithfield. Includes post-shoot edit review session. Cost: £45 members / £65 non-members.

### May 11 — Lightroom Mastery
Online workshop, 10am–1pm. Covers the full editing workflow from import to export, colour grading, and local adjustments. Cost: £35 members / £50 non-members.

### May 25 — Wildlife Photography Field Trip
Led by **Marcus Williams** at the Wicklow Mountains National Park. Full day, 6am–6pm. Covers fieldcraft, long-lens technique, and ethical wildlife photography. Limited to 8 participants. Cost: £95 members / £135 non-members.

### June 8 — Introduction to Darkroom Printing
At our darkroom facility. Half day, 10am–2pm. Learn to make fibre-based black and white prints from film negatives. Cost: £75 members (includes materials).

## Registration

Workshops fill quickly. Register through the member portal or email workshops@apertureclub.org. A 50% deposit secures your place.
