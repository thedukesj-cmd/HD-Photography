The theme *Into the Light* drew an extraordinary response from club members. Over 120 entries were submitted and our panel of judges spent three sessions selecting these twelve exceptional images.

Light is the raw material of photography — yet these photographers demonstrate how endlessly varied its possibilities remain. From the first soft minutes after sunrise to the violent brilliance of a summer storm breaking, each image here captures a moment that cannot be repeated.
