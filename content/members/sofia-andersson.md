Sofia Andersson travels 200+ days a year chasing authentic human stories. Based in Stockholm, her work documents everything from remote Himalayan villages to bustling Asian megacities.

Her debut book *Faces of the Silk Road* was published in 2023 and shortlisted for the Deutsche Börse Photography Foundation Prize.
