Marcus Okafor trained as a darkroom printer before transitioning to digital. His work retains the tonal richness of analogue processes, blending classical composition with contemporary subjects.

Solo exhibitions include *Negative Space* (London, 2022) and *Grey Scale* (Lagos, 2023).
