Elena Vasquez is an award-winning urban photographer based in Barcelona. With over a decade behind the lens, she specialises in finding extraordinary beauty in the everyday rhythms of city life.

Her work has been featured in *National Geographic Traveller*, *Photography Week*, and numerous international exhibitions. She teaches composition workshops at the Barcelona Photography Institute.
