James Chen has spent fifteen years photographing endangered species across four continents. His patient, unobtrusive approach allows him to capture intimate moments that would otherwise go unwitnessed.

Collaborating with conservation organisations worldwide, James uses photography as a tool for environmental advocacy and education.
